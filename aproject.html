Architectural Visualization Project - Details

As part of my Graphics, Design, and Animation subject, I created a 2D architectural visualization of a simple modern dream house. The project involved designing floor plans and elevations that capture the essence of a modern, minimalist home. Using graphic design software, I focused on clean lines, open spaces, and functional layout, showcasing the house’s contemporary aesthetic. This project allowed me to demonstrate my skills in 2D design, layout, and architectural presentation, providing a clear and visually appealing representation of a dream home.

Parteach Android App - Details

For one of my projects, I developed PARTEACH, a mobile app designed to improve communication between parents and teachers by tracking student attendance. The app allows parents to easily check if their child is present, absent, or late for class, giving them real-time access to their child’s attendance status. With a user-friendly interface, PARTEACH helps strengthen the connection between parents and teachers, ensuring better support for the student’s academic progress.

Multimedia Projects - Details

During my coursework, I had the chance to work on several Multimedia Projects that focused on integrating various forms of media, such as visuals, sound, and animation, to create cohesive and interactive content. These projects allowed me to develop a deeper understanding of how different media elements can come together to enhance storytelling, user experience, and communication. Through hands-on work, I honed my skills in video editing, graphic design, and animation, creating impactful multimedia presentations.
